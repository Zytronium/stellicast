# Stellicast Site-Wide Content Rules

_<small>Last updated: March 29, 2026</small>_

While Stellicast encourages free speech, we enforce certain site-wide rules to keep the platform safe and enjoyable for everyone.

## Site-Wide Rules

1. <span className="text-xl">Do not **break the law**</span>  
Do not violate any applicable law, regulation, or court order. Do not infringe or misappropriate any third-party rights (including copyright, trademark, or privacy rights).

2. <span className="text-xl">Do not **upload harmful, abusive, or explicit content**</span>   
Do not post content that is illegal, fraudulent, or deceptive. Do not post hateful, harassing, threatening, or violence-inciting material. Do not post sexually explicit or suggestive content of any kind, including any depiction of minors in a sexual context. Do not post excessively graphic or violent content.

3. <span className="text-xl">Do not **exploit or harm others**</span>   
Do not engage in bullying, stalking, doxxing, or targeted harassment of any individual or group. Do not impersonate any real person or entity, or misrepresent your affiliation with one.

4. <span className="text-xl">Do not **compromise security or integrity**</span>   
Do not attempt to gain unauthorized access to the Service or to other users' accounts. Do not distribute malware, viruses, or other malicious code. Do not interfere with or disrupt the normal functioning of the Service.

5. <span className="text-xl">Do not **circumvent technical measures**</span>   
Do not bypass, disable, or interfere with any security features or content protections without our explicit permission. Do not use automated systems (bots, scrapers, etc.) to access or collect data from the Service in ways that burden our infrastructure or violate our policies.

6. <span className="text-xl">Do not **misuse AI content**</span>   
Do not misrepresent AI-generated content as entirely human-made where our policy requires disclosure. Do not use AI or automation to mass-generate spam, low-quality content, or deceptive material.

7. <span className="text-xl">***Do*** **follow sector rules when uploading content**</span>   
When uploading or moving content to a sector, follow the sector rules. In the event a sector rule conflicts with a site-wide rule, the site-wide rules take precedence.

Stellicast reserves the right—but not the obligation*—to review, moderate, or remove User Content or accounts that are believed to violate these Terms or applicable law. Sector moderators also share responsibility for enforcing sector and site-wide rules on their sectors. Active sectors that do not reliably enforce rules will be terminated. Sectors whose core use or purpose breaks site-wide rules or terms of use will be terminated.

\* While we will try our best to moderate content, we cannot guarantee that we will always be able to do so.

---

## Moderation and Enforcement

We may enforce these Terms in various ways, including:

- Removing or restricting access to specific content.
- Limiting certain account features or functionality.
- Suspending or terminating accounts, channels, or sectors.
- Reporting illegal content or activity to relevant authorities when required or appropriate.
