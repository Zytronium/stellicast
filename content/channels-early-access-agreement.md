# Channels Early Access Agreement

_<small>Last updated: June 20, 2026</small>_

By applying to Early Access for Channels, you agree to the following:

You will follow platform-wide [rules](/rules) and Stellicast's [Terms of Use](/terms-of-use) while using channels and 
all other aspects of the website.

Key reminders:
- You are responsible for all content you upload and must have the rights or permission to upload it
- Do not upload illegal, harmful, explicity, hateful, or deceptive content
- Videos with significant AI-generated content must be labeled as AI when uploading
- Do not use AI to mass produce low-quality or low-effort content
- Do not break sector rules when posting to sectors
- Follow common sense, don't upload it if you think it's against the spirit of the rules and terms
- Unlisted and private videos are subject to the same rules and terms as public content and may be viewed by platform moderators if flagged or reported.
