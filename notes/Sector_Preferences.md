## Open Posting = false
only members who have been a member for at least 1 hour can post videos to this sector

## Private Access = true
Sector is invite-only and users must either be invited or request and be approved to join. Only approved members and Stellicast moderators can view content here

## Approval for posting = true
(new setting not yet implemented)
All videos but be approved by sector mods before being publicly visible. Sector mods can create a role that makes their videos auto-approved

## Allow AI Content = false
When uploading a video and selecting a sector with this preference, if the "Contains AI" checkbox later becomes checked, this video cannot be uploaded until it is unchecked.
If the "Contains AI" checkbox is already checked when choosing sectors to upload to, sectors with this preference will not appear in the sector search.
This measure takes a level of trust with the user to not lie about AI content in order to circumvent this rule.

## Min Video Length and Max Video Length
When uploading a video to (a) sector(s), the site should run a video length validation check, ensuring it falls between
the min and max video durations for ALL selected sectors. When selecting sectors, this check should NOT determine which
sectors appear, however, when the check runs and does not pass on one or more sectors selected, it should highlight the
appropriate sector(s) in red and show an error message explaining the issue with video length in this/these sector(s).
